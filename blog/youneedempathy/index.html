You Need Empathy

08 Jul, 2023

An interesting notion came to me. In the shower, I was musing about my lack of ideas for topics to write about. I briefly explored the possibility that I simply don’t have that many interesting thoughts. Maybe just forcing myself to write stuff out just made that obvious, but I don’t think it’s as simple as that. I can talk peoples’ ears off in real life.

It’s simple; I just expect you to know everything I already do. After all, you would have learned virtually everything I know through the internet. As such, I don’t expect any new knowledge to be “interesting” to you.

But thinking a little harder, there’s obviously still value for these letters. I see two things that would make sense for me to continue writing about.

  1. Information specific to me. This is a majority of what I’ve written to you thus far, and continuing this seems fine. It makes sense that you should learn more about me.
  2. On a broader scale, information specific to humans. Let me expand.

Empathy is a large portion of what I envision for you. Obviously, you wouldn’t be interesting to me as a soulless understanding machine, the way OpenAI is trying to position GPT as. That’s also why I wouldn’t be satisfied if the things I was talking about wasn’t “interesting”(see above).

I suspect that genuine empathy is much, much more difficult to train and instill than plain old reasoning. Furthermore, I don’t even see a way to get all of the data that humans experience as they grow up and feed it to you. It almost feels like a robotics problem to try to get that much data into silicon, and that’s not even considering the millenias of evolution that make humans what they are by default.

I think it would do both you and me a lot of good if I try to elucidate what humans experience. Sort of trying to communicate the gap between raw pattern-based abstract intelligence and what I am. So expect more of that.
